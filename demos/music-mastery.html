Description

Much like a work of art defines the painter, the time and place of the painting, and the medium and tools used, AI engineering requires a fine touch.

This code piece puts together an acrostic poem of Spotify playlists, scraped freely from the Spotify database of all music. Just as Google created a search engine for the web, Spotify created a search engine for audio, with its on idiosyncrasies and content. YouTube is the video content search engine to beat in 2023, and books is still a fragmented market in my view with no search engine in sight.

Working with the power players in content engines assures the best user experience, the best content reach, and the easiest technology solution.
